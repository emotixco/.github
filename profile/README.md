### What we're building

Emotix is a B2B platform where AI agents collaborate to validate startup ideas. You describe your concept — our agents handle the rest:

- **Market Research** — sizing, trends, and opportunity mapping
- **Competitor Intelligence** — feature comparison, pricing analysis, positioning gaps
- **Product Strategy** — PRDs, user personas, wireframes, and go-to-market plans
- **Financial Modeling** — unit economics, revenue projections, fundraising benchmarks

Built in Estonia. Used by **260+ founders** across pre-seed to Series A.
